🐉 Binary Garden

a system where motion reveals meaning

what this is

a small interactive system.

it looks simple.

but it explores:

  • movement as learning
  • systems that respond instead of instructing
  • how meaning accumulates through interaction

core idea

what you carry forward matters.

this is not a game about winning.

it is a space for:

  • observing
  • interacting
  • noticing what changes

dragon ring

binary garden is part of a larger system.

a learning environment where:

  • motion reveals structure
  • feedback replaces instruction
  • understanding emerges through interaction

notes
