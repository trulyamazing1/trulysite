🐉 Binary Garden

a system where motion reveals meaning

what this is

Binary Garden is a small interactive system.

It looks simple, but it explores movement as learning, systems that respond instead of instructing, and meaning that accumulates through interaction.

core idea

what you carry forward matters.

This is not a game about winning.

It is a space for observing, interacting, and noticing what changes.

dragon ring

Binary Garden is part of a larger system.

A learning environment where motion reveals structure, feedback replaces instruction, and understanding emerges through interaction.

notes

This project is evolving.

Some systems are visible. Some are not yet revealed.

The dragon does not tell you what to do. It shows you what changed.
